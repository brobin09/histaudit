How it works

Enter your courses

Add the courses you\u2019ve taken and plan to take. Titles and credit hours fill in automatically, and each course is checked against the requirement it\u2019s placed under.

Watch your progress

General Education, Major Core, and Electives tally live against the 120 hours you need to graduate. Misplaced courses and gaps are flagged as you go.

Add your goals and internships

Record where you want history to take you \u2014 career plans, graduate school, internships and opportunities \u2014 so advising is about your whole path, not just the checklist.

Export for advising

When you\u2019re ready, export one file and bring it to your advisor. That same file is your backup \u2014 keep it somewhere safe, like a cloud folder.

Your work stays yours

Degree Path runs entirely in your own browser. Nothing you enter is sent anywhere or stored on any server \u2014 not your grades, not your Banner ID, not your goals. This page cannot receive your data even if it wanted to.

Because your work lives in the browser, clearing your browser data would erase it. The exported file is the real save, so export whenever you\u2019ve made changes.

On a shared or lab computer?

Since the browser holds your work, the next person to open Degree Path on that same machine could see it. Two ways to protect yourself:

  • Open this page in a private / incognito window. It forgets everything the moment you close it \u2014 just export your file before you do.
  • Or when you finish, export your file and then click Clear My Data in the top bar.
